Urban planning · housing · mobility · climate adaptation · regional governance

Research, teaching, and community partnerships for equitable urban futures.

I study how housing systems, transportation access, environmental risk, public finance, and community institutions shape life chances for marginalized communities. My work connects rigorous research with planning practice, public-sector collaboration, and education across graduate, undergraduate, and high-school pathways.

This site presents my research program, teaching, professional practice, public scholarship, and student collaborations. It is designed as a narrative portfolio, not a document repository.

Research program

Planning for opportunity, access, and resilience across fragmented urban regions.

My research examines institutions and spatial systems that shape who can access stable housing, mobility, services, and community support. Across projects, I ask how local decisions, public investments, and regional interdependencies structure opportunity and vulnerability.
