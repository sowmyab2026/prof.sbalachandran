Course

Law, Ethics, and Practice in Planning

A professional practice course focused on legal standards, ethical decision-making, social justice, community expectations, and the responsibilities of planners.

Course themes

  • Ethical decision-making in planning.
  • Professional norms and responsibilities.
  • AICP ethical principles and professional conduct.
  • Planning law and public decision-making.
  • Equity planning and social responsibility.

Practice orientation

The course uses case studies, role play, reflective writing, public communication, and applied scenarios to prepare students for professional planning practice.
