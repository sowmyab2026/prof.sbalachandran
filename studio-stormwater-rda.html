Studio 2024-2025

Stormwater Regulation and Green Infrastructure Implementation

Applied planning work supporting implementation of the Residual Designation Authority permit program in Boston.

Overview

The studio worked with Boston's Department of Green Infrastructure to examine how regulatory mandates can be translated into practical, equitable, and scalable solutions for managing stormwater runoff from large impervious properties.

Students produced inventories, typologies, pilot site analyses, green infrastructure catalogs, and implementation strategies.
