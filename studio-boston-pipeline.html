Studio 2023-2024

Advancing Pathways to Equity in Urban Planning

Evaluation and implementation planning for the Boston Pipeline Initiative and pathways into the planning profession for youth of color.

Overview

The studio evaluated, strengthened, and institutionalized an inter-institutional effort to expand pathways for youth of color into the planning profession. Students used program evaluation, stakeholder engagement, comparative case studies, community mapping, network analysis, and co-design workshops.
