Professional practice

Planning leadership before and alongside academia.

My professional practice includes social entrepreneurship, affordable housing strategy, planning consulting, and international work across land management, statutory planning, sanitation, transit-oriented development, and post-disaster reconstruction.

Founded initiative

Griha Pravesh Housing Facilitation Center

Established in 2010

Griha Pravesh was conceived as a first-of-its-kind housing facilitation center aimed at enabling low-income households to make informed choices about homeownership and navigate the process of buying. The initiative supported families through financial literacy, case management, and homebuyer facilitation.

The work was shortlisted for the annual P3 Impact Award in 2018.

Co-founder

DBS Affordable Home Strategy

Registered in 2009

DBS Affordable Homes was established to demonstrate market-driven solutions for affordable housing in India. I led market assessments, product mix strategies, and development planning for more than 4,000 homes across three cities in Gujarat. The company was acquired by Brick Eagle in 2014.

Co-founder

Alchemy Urban Systems Pvt. Ltd.

Registered in August 2007

Alchemy Urban Systems is a professional planning consulting firm that prepared plans for cities and regions in India, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Mauritius, and Mexico. The firm specialized in land management, transit-oriented development, post-disaster reconstruction, city-wide sanitation, basic needs planning, and land-use planning.
