Professional practice and public scholarship

Planning leadership across public, nonprofit, and private-sector contexts.

My professional practice includes social entrepreneurship, affordable housing strategy, international planning consulting, and public-facing research products.

Founded initiative

Griha Pravesh Housing Facilitation Center

Established in 2010, Griha Pravesh was conceived as a first-of-its-kind housing facilitation center aimed at enabling low-income households to make informed choices about homeownership and navigate the process of buying.

Read the U.S. Department of State P3 Impact Award finalist announcement

Co-founder

DBS Affordable Home Strategy

Registered in 2009, DBS Affordable Homes demonstrated market-driven solutions for affordable housing in India. I led market assessments, product mix strategies, and development planning for more than 4,000 homes across three cities in Gujarat.

View an affordable housing project brochure

Co-founder

Alchemy Urban Systems Pvt. Ltd.

Registered in 2007, Alchemy Urban Systems prepared plans for cities and regions in India, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Mauritius, and Mexico, specializing in land management, transit-oriented development, sanitation, post-disaster reconstruction, basic needs planning, and land-use planning.
