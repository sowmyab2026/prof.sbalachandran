Fair housing + LIHTC

Fair Housing Litigation and Tax Credit Policy in Texas

Research on how fair housing litigation shaped Qualified Allocation Plans, LIHTC siting incentives, revitalization criteria, and definitions of opportunity in Texas.

Overview

This project analyzes the evolution of Texas Qualified Allocation Plans from the early 2000s through the post-litigation period, focusing on deconcentration incentives, revitalization criteria, community opposition, basis boosts, and neighborhood opportunity.

Project status

Manuscript in progress. Related outputs will be added as they become publicly available.
